Zeeland Driver testing

 
 
 
 
 

Preparing to take your road test:

What to Bring to the Road Test (Under 18)

1.Your PERMIT (from the Secretary of State, received after completion of Segment I, it is white). You need to have been driving on the Permit for a total of six months (180 days) before you may take the Road Test. If you have a replacement Permit, the original date it was issued must be stamped on the permit.  If you have corr. lens on your permit, you must wear your glasses or contacts.

2.Your SEGMENT II certificate.

3.A ROADWORTHY vehicle with current registration and proof of current insurance. The following is a list of equipment that cannot be damaged, inoperative, or missing:

◦Horn

◦Muffler

◦Turn Signals

◦Safety Belts

◦Lug Nuts

◦Mirrors

◦Windshield

◦Tires

◦Brake Lights (including center brake light)

◦Speedometer

◦Washers


4.If you are renting a car from another company, please call us for further instructions.

5.A PARENT or legal guardian. PLEASE NOTE- The parent MUST ride along with the Tester and Applicant. No one else is allowed in the car during the test. Children are not allowed in the car, and animals are not allowed. Childcare is not provided.

6.Be ON TIME for your appointment. We suggest you arrive 5-10 minutes prior for your scheduled appointment time.  If you are unable to speak or understand ENGLISH, you must bring an interpreter of at least 16 years of age.

7.The cost of the Road Test is $55.00 payable at the time of the test.  We do need a 24-hour advance notice if you wish to change or cancel your appointment. There is NO REFUND for missed appointments or failures. We accept cash, or check.  ($25.00 charge for returned checks)

8.Failure to provide or comply with the first SEVEN steps will result in a $30.00 rescheduling fee.

9.The test takes about 45 minutes and consists of parking (including blindside backing a.k.a. parallel) city, highway and residential driving. Please refer to the Road Skills Test Study Guide link if you have further questions.


What to Bring to the Road Test (18 or Older)

  1. 1.Your PERMIT. You need to have your permit for a total of 30 days before you may take the Road Test.  If you have corr. lens on your permit, you must wear your glasses or contacts.

2.   A photo I.D.

   3.A ROADWORTHY vehicle with current registration and proof of current insurance. The following is a list of equipment that cannot be damaged, inoperative, or missing:

◦Horn

◦Muffler

◦Turn Signals

◦Safety Belts

◦Lug Nuts

◦Mirrors

◦Windshield

◦Tires

◦Brake Lights (including center brake light)

◦Speedometer

◦Washers


4.If you are renting a car from another company, please call us for further instructions.

5.Only the tester and applicant may ride along. Children are not allowed in the car. Childcare is not provided.

6.Be ON TIME for your appointment.  We suggest you arrive 5-10 minutes prior to your scheduled appointment time.

7.If you are unable to speak or understand ENGLISH, you must bring an interpreter of at least 16 years of age.

8.The cost of the Road Test is $55.00.  We do need a 24-hour advance notice if you wish to change or cancel your appointment. There is NO REFUND for missed appointments or failures. We accept cash, or check. ($25.00 charge for returned checks)

9.Failure to provide or comply with the first EIGHT steps will result in a $30.00 rescheduling fee.

  10. The test takes about 45 minutes and consists of parking (including blindside backing a.k.a. parallel) city, highway and residential driving.  Please refer to the Road Skills Test Study Guide link if you have further questions.



We MUST be able to clearly read the MICH DRIVER LICENSE number (ex. A 520 223 445 587)


       If you fail to bring all appropriate paperwork/permits or if your vehicle does not pass the safety inspection, you will be charged an additional $25 to reschedule.  If you are more than 5 minutes late for your appointment or if you fail to keep your appointment, the $55 fee is forfeited and you must pay an additional $55 to reschedule.  If you fail the test on the range, it is a $30 re-scheduling fee.  If you fail the test on the road, it is a $40 re-scheduling fee.
